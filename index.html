Who is WHO?

The World Health Organisation or WHO for short have been tracking the deadly pandemic covid-19 since the very begining of the outbreak.
According to WHO nearly every country in the world has had cases of the outbreak with three differnet strains of the virus known so far.

A brief WHO timeline
  • The Covid-19 virus was first detected in Wuhan China by WHO on the 31 December 2019.
  • The outbreak was offically declared a public health outbreak of international concern 30th of Janaury 2020.
